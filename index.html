Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Tiny Thunder technique?

Tiny Thunder is a method that uses 3rd party stories to present your ideas in a fresh, fun and powerfully persuasive way. This technique has been called “the most original marketing idea to emerge in the last 100 years.”

How quickly can I get my emails?

Within seconds! Once you have answered our simple questionnaire, you press a button and will get all your monthly emails immediately.

Are these emails just templates or will they be customized to my business?

Each email will be customized to your business, product and prospect. You will be given 100% original, story-based emails (complete with images) that have been written by world-class writers

Do you have a free trial?

Yes! You can try out Tiny Thunder for 5 days to see if it’s a great fit for you.

Can I change the number of emails I receive per month?

Yes of course! You can also increase or decrease the number of monthly emails you receive at any time by changing your subscription.

Do I have to commit to a year with Tiny Thunder?

No, you have the choice to buy a monthly or an annual subscription. You can end your subscription at any time. It will end once your current billing period is over.
